About me

Hi, my name is Dobrin! I was born and raised in Shumen, Bulgaria but am now living in Tatarevo - a village 50km away from Plovdiv. I graduated from "Nancho Popovich" high school of natural sciences and math after which I moved out to Sofia to have my bachelor's degree in Sound Engineering. I had a music band in Sofia for several years but I eventually left Sofia and moved to Plovdiv for a cool new job. I love nature and hiking in the mountains and in contrast to my city life in Sofia and Plovdiv, I'm now living the village life having rediscovered myself in gardening and in parenthood as I became a dad in December-2022.

I've always had an inclination towards programming but something always led me astray (I even briefly studied mathematics and informatics in Sofia University but dropped out because I wanted to do music). My current job was responsible for me realising that coding is something that is really interesting and exciting to me and a few months ago I started learning how to code. I'm really loving it so far and each and every aspect of it. Being able to build something out of nothing and see it come to life is really satisfying and it's not just the end product that I enjoy because I also love the entire process as well - starting from "how the hell am I gonna do that?!" all the way through to "oh, my God, it's finally working!" with each and every little obstacle and challenge all along the way and the satisfaction that comes from solving all the small (and big) problems. I'm loving it. Da hodim da kodim.
